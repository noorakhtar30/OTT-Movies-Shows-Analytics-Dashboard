## Data Source
Public OTT dataset containing structured data on:
- Movies and TV shows across multiple platforms  
- Release years and content eras  
- Ratings and viewer scores  
- Platform-wise content distribution  

## Business Problem
With the rapid growth of OTT platforms, stakeholders often lack a unified view to:
- Compare content libraries across platforms  
- Identify trends in content volume and ratings over time  
- Understand how different platforms position their content  

Raw platform-specific datasets make cross-platform analysis difficult.

## Dashboard Goals
- Enable comparison of OTT platforms using key KPIs  
- Analyze content trends by era, rating, and platform  
- Support data-driven decisions related to content strategy and market positioning  

## Key Features
- **KPI Cards:** Total titles, average ratings, platforms covered  
- **Platform Comparison:** Content distribution across Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, and Hulu  
- **Era Analysis:** Trends in content releases across different time periods  
- **Ratings Analysis:** Viewer rating distribution and performance comparison  
- **Interactive Filters:** Dynamic slicing by platform, era, and content type  

## Business Impact
- **Strategic Insights:** Compare OTT platforms at a market level  
- **Content Strategy:** Identify growth trends and rating performance  
- **Decision Support:** Enable executive-level, data-driven platform comparisons
